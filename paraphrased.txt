We are currently experiencing a revolution in the field of intelligence. Machines are acquiring the ability to think, reason and make decisions, which was previously believed to be exclusive to humans. The impact of AI can be observed in domains, such as self driving cars and voice activated assistants, where it is revolutionizing industries and simplifying our daily lives. However these advancements also bring about challenges and ethical considerations. As we delve further into the potential of AI it is crucial that we use this technology responsibly and ensure its benefits are accessible, to all of humanity.
